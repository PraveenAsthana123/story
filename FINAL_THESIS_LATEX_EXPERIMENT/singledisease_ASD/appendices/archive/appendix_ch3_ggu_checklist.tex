\addlinespace
\textbf{Qualitative Research} & 12-member expert panel; Likert-scale + open-ended responses; Salda\~{n}a three-stage coding; Krippendorff's $\alpha \geq 0.80$ threshold \\
\addlinespace
\textbf{Statistical Analysis} & Pre-registered: accuracy $\geq$85\%, DL$>$baselines ($p<.05$), SHAP top-3 features, automation equivalence ($p>.05$, $d<0.20$), RAG quality $\geq$80\% \\
\addlinespace
\textbf{Scope} & 5 public datasets; SVM, RF, GB, XGBoost, CNN, CNN-LSTM, EEGNet; Python~3.11, TF~2.14, scikit-learn~1.3.2; NVIDIA RTX~4090 GPU \\
\addlinespace
\textbf{What Was Not Considered} & Proprietary hospital data; real-time streaming inference; federated learning; transformer architectures (GPT-based diagnostics) \\
\addlinespace
\textbf{Limitations} & Retrospective benchmarks only; no IRB-approved clinical trial; expert panel convenience sample; single-GPU reproducibility \\
\addlinespace
\textbf{Future Scope} & Prospective multi-site validation; real-time edge deployment; transformer integration; federated privacy-preserving training \\
\addlinespace
\textbf{APA~7th Edition} & Methods reported with sufficient detail for replication; tables follow APA format; statistical notation per APA (e.g., \textit{p}, \textit{d}, CI); all instruments described \\
