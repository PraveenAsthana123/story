\noindent\textbf{\color{ggunavy}3. Key Strengths}

\begin{itemize}[nosep]
\item \textbf{Exceptional intellectual honesty:} The chapter explicitly distinguishes what has been demonstrated from what has been theorised (Table~5.16: Organizational Validation Status). Counter-arguments (Section~5.12) anticipate and address five specific examiner objections with candid responses.
\item \textbf{Comprehensive theoretical anchoring:} Each hypothesis interpretation is grounded in established theory (RBV, TAM, STS, DSR, UTAUT) with specific citations, and the technology-to-adoption bridge table (Table~5.15) makes the mapping explicit and transparent.
\item \textbf{Strong governance operationalisation:} The KPI escalation protocol (Table~5.9), four-level escalation ladder (Figure~5.5), and governance maturity model (Table~5.7) translate abstract governance concepts into concrete decision rules with thresholds, response times, and accountability paths.
\item \textbf{Thorough appendix forward referencing:} Nine explicit \texttt{Appendix\textasciitilde\textbackslash ref\{\}} forward references connect the chapter to supplementary material, enabling examiners to drill into detail without cluttering the main narrative.
\item \textbf{Robust conclusion:} The chapter ends with a bullet-point summary of eight principal findings, followed by a bold thesis argument sentence that synthesises the entire dissertation, and a closing reflective statement.
\end{itemize}
