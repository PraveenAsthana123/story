\noindent\textbf{Detailed Assessments:}

\begin{enumerate}[nosep, leftmargin=*]
\item \textbf{Rule 1 (Pass):} The chapter overview opens with ``This chapter argues that\ldots''\ phrasing, consistent with the playbook first-principle requirement. The bold thesis-argument statement at the chapter conclusion reinforces the same argumentative stance.

\item \textbf{Rule 2 (Pass):} Most sections open with a claim or synthesis statement before presenting tables. For example, Section~\ref{sec:practice_lit} opens with ``This section synthesizes empirical literature across six themes'' before any table.

\item \textbf{Rule 3 (Pass):} Sections consistently follow the 1-3-1 pattern (e.g., Theoretical Foundations, Hypothesis Development, Empirical Literature). The Empirical Literature section uses a tiered structure with clear groupings, maintaining readability and structural coherence throughout.

\item \textbf{Rule 4 (Pass):} All tables have introduction sentences before them and interpretation paragraphs after them. Tables~\ref{tab:transfer_learning_gains}, \ref{tab:validation_prevalence}, and \ref{tab:reproducibility_indicators} now include explicit post-table interpretation paragraphs stating what the data means for the study.

\item \textbf{Rule 5 (Pass):} Sections consistently open with roadmap sentences. Section~\ref{sec:lit_search_method} opens with ``This review adopts an integrative approach\ldots''; Section~\ref{sec:conceptual_foundations} opens with ``Table\textasciitilde\textbackslash ref\{tab:construct\_summary\} defines the five core constructs\ldots''

\item \textbf{Rule 6 (Pass):} All sections end with effective transition bridges (e.g., Section~\ref{sec:gaps} to contributions; Chapter Recap to Chapter~3). Subsections within the Empirical Literature section include explicit bridging sentences connecting thematic topics in sequence.

\item \textbf{Rule 7 (Pass):} Sentences consistently target the $\leq$35 word limit. Long compound sentences in the opening paragraph, RAG discussion, and hypothesis development section have been restructured into shorter, focused statements that maintain scientific precision within the word target.

\item \textbf{Rule 8 (Pass):} All paragraphs stay within the 200-word target. The Responsible AI Frameworks and Ensemble Methods paragraphs have been tightened to comply with the word limit while preserving analytical depth.

\item \textbf{Rule 9 (Pass):} Numerical data is presented in tables. Inline statistics (e.g., study proportions, methodological prevalence) are used sparingly and only where they directly support the narrative argument, with all systematic numerical data consolidated into dedicated tables.

\item \textbf{Rule 10 (Pass):} All 57 cross-references use the \texttt{Table\textasciitilde\textbackslash ref\{\}} and \texttt{Figure\textasciitilde\textbackslash ref\{\}} pattern. No vague references (``the table below,'' ``the figure above'') were found.

\item \textbf{Rule 11 (Pass):} The chapter ends with: (a)~a bullet-point recap (Section~\ref{sec:ch2_recap}, 6 bullets), (b)~a summary table (Table~\ref{tab:ch2_summary}), (c)~a bold thesis argument statement, and (d)~an implicit bridge to Chapter~3. All four elements are present and well-executed.

\item \textbf{Rule 12 (Pass):} The chapter references appendix content in 27 locations, including ``Appendix\textasciitilde\textbackslash ref\{app:ch2\_asd\}'' through ``\textbackslash ref\{app:ch2\_wearable\}'' and multiple ``MOVED TO APPENDIX'' comments. The Per-Topic Literature Scope subsection (lines~909--922) provides a consolidated appendix directory.

\item \textbf{Rule 13 (Pass):} The chapter presents three primary theoretical frameworks (RBV, TAM, DSR) with six supporting frameworks in a clearly tiered structure. The tiered presentation meets the $\leq$3 core frameworks target while preserving the theoretical breadth needed for a ASD-focused literature review.
\end{enumerate}

\noindent\textbf{\color{ggunavy}3. Key Strengths}

\begin{itemize}[nosep]
\item \textbf{Systematic methodology:} PRISMA flow diagram with explicit screening numbers (2,847 identified $\to$ 156 included), quality appraisal scoring, and thematic saturation reporting at 120 studies.
\item \textbf{Gap-to-contribution traceability:} Every gap (G1--G6) is mapped to a specific contribution (C1--C6) with theory backing and DBA relevance, creating a verifiable chain of evidence.
\item \textbf{Contradictory evidence documented:} Five contradictions are explicitly analysed (DL vs.\ baselines, sample size, explainability paradox, automation acceptance, publication bias), demonstrating intellectual honesty.
\item \textbf{Multi-level hypothesis model:} The H0--H7 mediation model with falsifiability anchor (H0), moderation (H6), and competing direct path (H7) exceeds the rigour expected of a standard DBA literature review.
\item \textbf{Management integration:} Strategic field analyses (SWOT, CMM, Blue Ocean), KPI mapping, trust framework, and ethical AI considerations bridge the technical and organisational dimensions expected in a DBA.
\end{itemize}
