LLD 3. Low-Level Design

3.1 EEG Preprocessing Pipeline

RAW EEG --> Signal Loading --> Segmentation (2-4s @ 256Hz) --> Feature Extraction (47 features) --> NaN Handling --> Train/Test Split (5-Fold) --> Augmentation (50->200) --> Standardization --> Ultra Stacking Ensemble (15 clf)
