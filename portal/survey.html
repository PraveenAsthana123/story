About This Research Project

RGAIG+ (Responsible Generative AI Governance) is a doctoral research framework that unifies AI diagnostic accuracy with regulatory governance for multi-domain biomedical diagnosis using consumer EEG wearables. Developed at Golden Gate University (DBA, June 2026) by Praveen K Asthana, it addresses a critical gap: no existing framework governs the entire pipeline from wearable device signal capture through AI-driven diagnosis to patient-facing explainability under a single compliance architecture.
